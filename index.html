About the Webinar

Step into the world of classical elegance with Supriya Kelkar 🌸. Join this beautifully crafted Prarambhik (Basic) session to explore the soulful expressions, rhythm, and foundational postures that define Bharatanatyam. Whether you’re a beginner or simply curious about Indian classical dance, this webinar will help you experience the discipline, grace, and devotion behind every movement.

In this interactive session, you’ll gain:
Insight into the heritage and significance of Bharatanatyam
Hands-on guidance on basic postures and movements
Understanding of rhythm (Tala) and expression (Abhinaya)
Live Q&A to discuss techniques and training paths
Connect with dance enthusiasts and practitioners globally
Certificate of Participation upon completion

Meet Your Instructor

Supriya Kelkar

Bharatanatyam Artist & Instructor

With over 25 years of experience in Bharatanatyam, Supriya Kerkar has performed at prestigious venues across the globe, sharing the stage with eminent artists such as Hema Malini and her revered guru, Mrs. Tamanna Nair. A Bharatanatyam Visharad from the Akhil Bhartiya Gandharva Mahavidyalaya, Supriya completed her formal training in 1999. & Bcom in 2003 Having taught and mentored over 500 students, she continues to uphold the classical traditions of Bharatanatyam while bringing in fresh, contemporary interpretations that make her teaching both rooted and relevant.
