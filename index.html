About the Webinar

In this immersive session, acclaimed Bharatanatyam artist Supriya Kelkar will guide you through the fundamentals of this ancient Indian classical dance form. Discover the intricate hand gestures (mudras), facial expressions (abhinaya), and rhythmic footwork that make Bharatanatyam a complete storytelling art. The webinar includes live demonstrations, interactive Q&A sessions, and opportunities to connect with fellow dance enthusiasts.

What You'll Get:
6+ hours of instructional content
Downloadable resources and practice guides
Live Q&A sessions with Supriya Kelkar
Certificate of participation

Meet Your Instructor

Supriya Kelkar

Bharatanatyam Artist & Instructor

With over 15 years of experience in Bharatanatyam, Supriya has performed at prestigious venues worldwide and has taught hundreds of students. Trained under renowned gurus, she brings both traditional technique and contemporary interpretation to her teaching.
